How this is wired

Architecture, briefly

  • FRONTEND This page, served directly by the same pod it stresses — no separate frontend host.
  • BACKEND A small Flask app (app.py) behind gunicorn, exposing /api/stress/cpu and /api/stress/memory.
  • SCALING A HorizontalPodAutoscaler watches CPU utilization on the Deployment and scales 2 → 6 replicas at a 70% target.
  • ROUTING A Service + ingress-nginx Ingress route static-website.local to whichever pod is available — including newly scaled ones.
